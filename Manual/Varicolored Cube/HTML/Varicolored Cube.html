On the Subject of Varicolored Cube

Q&A, but the questions are really dumb...

This module contains a Colored Cube, which can be either (R)ed, (G)reen, (B)lue, (Y)ellow, (M)agenta, (C)yan, (W)hite or Blac(K). Exactly one pair of opposite faces is selectable (either the left and right faces are selectable, or the back and front).

In this module, you will be answering questions about a certain subject, which can be a number, a letter, a color or a direction. Use the color of the cube and the number in the top left corner of the cube’s top face in the table below to obtain that subject (colors have thicker borders compared to letters/other cells; directions are represented by arrows).

R G B Y M C W K
1 B ⭧ M ⭠ 9 W ⭢ ⭠
2 N 5 16 ⭩ Z W K 2
3 15 4 10 ⭡ R I ⭩ X
4 8 V F ⭨ 14 P R C
5 Y ⭨ A 6 G M ⭣ ⭦
6 L Q 1 K ⭣ B 3 ⭢
7 12 Y U ⭧ ⭦ C D 13
8 B G K S G 11 7 ⭡

Once you find your subject, press either of the selectable faces. The left/right faces pair will now be the selectable pair of faces.

Using the new color of the cube and the number in the top left corner of the cube’s top face, obtain a question about the subject in the table on the next page. If the question correctly asks about the subject, press the left face to accept the question, otherwise, press the right face to reject the question.
