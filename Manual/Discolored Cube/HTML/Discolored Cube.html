On the Subject of Discolored Cube

What’s all this about stripping???

This module contains a Colored Cube, which can be either (R)ed, (G)reen, (B)lue, (Y)ellow, (M)agenta or (C)yan. Exactly one of the left/right faces is selectable, and exactly one of the front/back faces is selectable.

Additionally, every strip has a value associated with it, also obtained from the table. This shouldn’t be confused with the strip’s index, which is the strip’s position in the module’s list (ranging from 1 to 6).

On the module, the number in the top left corner of the cube’s top face will always be the index of the strip that you are currently on. Initially, and only initially, the color of the cube will be the color at your current position in the strip you are on.

Pressing the left/right face will move you to the previous (when pressing the left face) or the next (when pressing the right face) color in the strip, wrapping around to the start/end of the strip if needed.

Pressing the front/back face will move you to the previous (when pressing the front face) or the next (when pressing the back face) strip in order, wrapping around to the first/last strip if needed. Your position in the strip will stay the same. Do not move to the next/previous strip 6 times in a row while maintaining the same position within the strips before figuring out the strip’s valid colors.

Whenever you move from one strip to another, the color that you were on at the first strip will append (if the selectable face is the right face) or prepend (if the selectable face is the left face) to the first strip and then become the cube’s new color.

Once you determine all of the module’s strips and their values, determine the valid colors for each strip:

  1. Take the strip’s index, subtract it from 7, and look at the strip with that index.
  2. Take the new strip’s value, take it modulo 6, and add 1.
  3. The color in the position equal to the result of the previos step in the initial strip is its valid color.

Make it so that there is a position N, where for each strip position N contains the strip’s valid color. Navigate to position N in the first (if the selectable face is the back face) or the last (if the selectable face is the front face) strip, then move to the next/previous strip 6 times to submit your answer. If done correctly, the module will solve. Otherwise, a strike will be given, and the module will not reset.
